Hiking Trip gone a bit wild...

Images from the adventure

this was a trip that happened a few months ago with my friend JC & Nila

It was on Mount Mansfield in Vermont

We planned to hike Mount Mansfield and leave quite early in the morning. Our hiking group ended up leaving later than expected (we expected to leave at ~8AM, but actually left at noon. According to a french review on the Mansfield All Trails, the hike should have only taken 4-5 hours...

When we arrived, we spoke with a person who worked for the state park and he let us know that hiking Mansfield would take 8-12 hours.

We decided to hike along a different section of the trail...

We accidentally ended up on the actual Trail and then it got quite dark.

Our friends Deet & Dev helped us out though & it ended up working out! :)
